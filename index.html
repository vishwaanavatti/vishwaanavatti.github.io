Ready Mix Concrete

We supply industry-standard ready-mixed concrete tailored to structural requirements with lab-tested mixes and certified quality control.

Standard Mix Types

  • M10, M15, M20, M25 — Common workability mixes
  • M30, M35, M40 — High strength structural mixes
  • Grade-specific designs: Pumpable, Slump-controlled, RCC, Pavement mixes
  • Special mixes: High early strength, Low heat, Lightweight, Self Compacting Concrete (SCC)

Mix designs follow IS 456 and IS 10262 guidelines and are tailored on request.

Transport & Pumping

We provide complete transport and concrete placement services tailored for busy construction sites. Key offerings include:

  • Fleet of transit mixers — modern, regularly maintained mixers of varying capacities to match project volumes and ensure consistent mix delivery.
  • Line pumps and boom pumps (boom services) — flexible line pumps for short reaches and high-capacity boom pumps for tall or long-reach pours, reducing manual handling and speeding placement.
  • Mobile pump fleet & qualified operators — certified pump operators and crew trained in safe, efficient placement techniques to maintain concrete quality on site.
  • On-site batching coordination — close coordination with batching plants to deliver the exact mix designs, slump control and temperature-management for hot-weather placements.
  • Transport scheduling & logistics — route planning, GPS tracking, and just-in-time dispatch to minimize waiting times and ensure timely arrival for continuous pours.
  • Placement support for complex pours — pour sequencing, pump siting, and placement supervision for high-volume, rapid-curing or specialized concreting works.
  • Preventive maintenance & uptime guarantees — scheduled servicing and spare-equipment readiness to reduce downtime during critical pours.
  • Safety & quality checks — on‑site slump testing, cylinder sampling and adherence to safety procedures and traffic/permit requirements for urban sites.
  • 24/7 emergency & high-volume support — extended-hour operations and surge capacity for tight timelines or urgent project needs.

Technical Services

Our technical services support designers and site teams through the full concrete lifecycle. Key services include:

  • Mix design & trial mixes — laboratory-backed designs tailored for strength, workability and durability requirements.
  • On-site testing — slump tests, temperature monitoring and cylinder sampling with rapid reporting.
  • Strength monitoring & lab testing — compressive strength, permeability and chloride diffusion testing through accredited labs.
  • Admixture & materials consultancy — recommendations for superplasticizers, retarders, accelerators and SCMs to meet project objectives.
  • Durability & specification review — service-life assessments, cover recommendations and exposure-class guidance.
  • Troubleshooting & corrective actions — site diagnostics for segregation, bleeding, early-age cracking and remedial approaches.
  • Instrumentation & digital reporting — sensor-based temperature/curing logs and digital delivery of test certificates and QA records.
  • Training & site support — on-site training for pump operators, supervisors and QC technicians to maintain placement quality.

Better Practices

We help projects adopt best practices that improve performance, reduce lifecycle costs and lower environmental impact:

  • Thermal control & curing — temperature management, insulated formwork and recommended curing regimes for optimal strength gain.
  • Water-cement ratio management — controls and testing to ensure designed strength and durability are achieved on-site.
  • Placement sequencing & consolidation — guidance on pour sequencing, compaction and vibration to avoid defects.
  • Sustainable material sourcing — use of SCMs, recycled aggregates and low-carbon options where appropriate.
  • Formwork & finishing guidance — recommendations for surface finish, shutter release and protection during early ages.
  • Waste reduction & resource planning — batch sizing and logistics to minimise returns and material waste.
  • QA/QC workflows — standardized checklists, acceptance criteria and documentation practices to streamline inspections.
  • Environmental & safety controls — dust, runoff and traffic management plans for urban placements.
